Stories of Saya

Every surprise tells a story. Families separated by distance still find a way to say, "Mahal kita." Through Knock Knock Saya, every gift, bouquet, and food tray becomes a warm embrace that brings joy into Filipino homes.

A Daughter's Birthday Gift for Tatay

A daughter working far from home booked a Knock Knock Saya birthday surprise for her father. When the team arrived with balloons, food, and flowers, the whole family smiled through happy tears. In that moment, distance disappeared—and love took its place.

Nanay's Unexpected Smile

A simple bouquet and thoughtful package turned an ordinary afternoon into an unforgettable Mother's Day. Nanay said it felt like receiving a hug from her children, even when they could not be physically present.

Love Shared at the Family Table

From cakes to complete surprise sets, families gather around what was delivered and celebrate together. Knock Knock Saya brings more than packages—it brings joy, connection, and cherished memories for Filipinos.
