About Knock Knock Saya

Knock Knock Saya is a surprise-gifting and celebration delivery service that helps Filipinos express love, even from afar. We turn ordinary days into unforgettable moments through curated Birthday, Mother’s Day, Father’s Day, and Valentine’s Day packages delivered with care.

Every package is prepared with heart—from flowers and cakes to personalized celebration bundles—so your loved ones feel seen, appreciated, and deeply loved. With easy ordering and dependable delivery, we make sure every “Knock Knock” becomes a moment of joy.

Our mission: to bring smiles, strengthen family connections, and spread love across Filipino homes—one surprise at a time.
